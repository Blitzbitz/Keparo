About Our Company

Keparo Enterprises is a Chingola-based leader in steel fabrication and industrial construction. Since our founding, we've built a reputation for precision metalwork and durable steel structures that stand the test of time in Zambia's demanding industrial environment.

Our state-of-the-art fabrication workshop features advanced CNC plasma cutting technology, enabling us to deliver complex steel components with millimeter precision for mining, agricultural, and commercial applications.
