About Our Company

Founded in Chingola, Keparo Enterprises has grown to become one of the most trusted construction firms in the Copperbelt region. We specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial construction projects with a commitment to quality craftsmanship and timely delivery.

Our team of certified professionals adheres to the highest Zambian construction standards and regulations. We are registered with the National Council for Construction (NCC) and fully compliant with all Zambian building codes and safety requirements.
